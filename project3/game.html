Instructions

Nockoffaruga is a simple broswer based game that relies on mouse control and 4 buttons. The objective is to maneuver a ship around the window and survive as long as possible by destroying enemies or dodging. Your score will increase naturally over time, but increases faster the longer the game lasts. Destroying ships will also increase your combo multiplier, which increases the amount of score you earn.
Enemy ships come in 4 different colors (red, blue, green and orange). In order to destroy them, you must run into them while your ship matches theirs in color. To change colors, use the Q, W, E, & R keys.

Q for blue
W for red
E for green
R for orange
